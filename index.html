00 summary/

[Mission Statement]
To humanize robotics and democratize automation by using art as a bridge between people and machines. To transform fear of technology into curiosity, empowerment, and collective ownership. Our goal: a cultural and emotional shift where robots belong to all of us—not just the few who can afford to control them.

[Concept Overview]
The exhibit merges sculpture, robotics, and wearable art into a seamless interactive environment. Visitors design, interact, and collaborate with robots as creative partners, dissolving the boundaries between art object and artificial intelligence. The result is a living dialogue about identity, automation, and what it means to be human in a mechanized world.

[Social Impact]
Familiarizing the Future reframes robots as approachable, customizable, and emotional. It challenges the fear narratives driven by monopolized tech culture and proposes a world where automation can redistribute wealth and free humans from monotonous labor. The exhibit promotes empathy, imagination, and ownership—showing that technology can serve humanity when shared collectively.
